CTE Makerspace: Agriculture

Making Flower Pots and Planters

Students!

Come explore the pot-in-pot planting technique of making a planter. No green thumb required! Receive valuable plant care tips while you learn how to plant, water and grow your own plant or flower.

Once done, you can bring your flower pot home to continue the growing!

The CTE Makerspace is a resource that offers hands-on learning and exploration workshops for career technical education (CTE) programs.

For more information, contact Christina Corrales-Cotto at ccorrales-cotto@collegeofthedesert.edu
