ABE Program

College of the Desert offers programs for individuals who are looking to earn a high school diploma, prepare to take the GED TEST or just take the GED TEST. COD also offers classes to learn to communicate; read, speak, and write in English for non-native speakers. Additionally, there is an ABE Counselor that will assist you to bridge over to pursue a college education, advance your workforce credentials and learn important life skills.
